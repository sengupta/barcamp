Scicamp Mumbai

Scicamp Mumbai is a free, barcamp style, participant lead conference that discusses science and technology.

A bunch of smart people meet up over the weekend, put up a schedule on a wall and spend the rest of their time taking up sessions and discussions with each other.

There is no audience. Only participants. So host a session, help out with planning, ask questions, spread the word- Everybody is invited. There really are no walls.
